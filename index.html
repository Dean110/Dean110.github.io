About Bosco

Bosco roamed the mean streets of Franklin County in search of adventure, love, and fulfillment. Once he had achieved all those things he was captured by a person just doing their job, the dog warden, and then adopted by the Williams-Schwieterman household.

Projects

Hole Under Neighbor's Fence

One of Bosco's finest efforts at date, this project is ongoing and faces many day to day challenges that Bosco strives to overcome. The neighbor's dog has been determined to be a threat to household security and Bosco continues to try to dig a covert tunnel into the neighbors yard to have a friendly conversation about chilling the heck out.

2017 - Current

Neighborhood Patrol

Bosco's true passion for life is best seen while patrolling the mean streets of the Eastmoor North community. Bosco tirelessly marks all trees along his perimeter checks of the neighborhood boundaries. He also maintains checks on the Eruv of Columbus to help out his Jewish comrades during the week.

2017- Current

Blanket Fort by Electric Fireplace

Bosco strives to maintain the comfiest of all bedding possibilities with his collection of worn blankets. He fashions intricate tunnels to maximize warm air flow to drive out un-comfy vibes from his basement chill spot.
